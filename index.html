Secondhand Styling: Good for the Planet, Bad for Capitalism

  • Next

Fast Fashion is Getting the Boot! In the past several years, secondhand fashion is making a big comeback. Here at Feminist Wears, we are all for it! Not only is secondhand shopping more sustainable, but it gives you the opportunity to find unique pieces that fit your personal style perfectly. Additionally, because the most sustainable option is to wear what you already own, a movement to be more playful and creative with your current wardrobe.

Benefits of a Wardrobe Refresh

  • Next

What Does Empowerment Through Fashion Mean? Often, fashion is pushed aside as a frivolous hobby, but learning about your personal style and what feels good on your body can have many positive effects. Getting dressed in clothes you love can:

  • Build Confidence
  • Spark Creativity
  • Start Your Morning Off Right
  • Appreciate Your Body

I am combining my passions of fashion and feminism to help others feel this empowerment. I've seen a huge shift in my self-esteem after deciding to wear the pieces in my wardrobe that I truly love.

How Can Feminist Wears Help?

Secondhand Styling Options We offer a variety of styling services to meet all your wardrobe needs. We focus on Mystery Thrift Boxes, Wardrobe Consultations, and Personal Shopping.

Mystery Thrift Box

Looking for a few new pieces to treat yourself to on a budget? This option is perfect for you! I will pick thrifted pieces for you based on your style and size preferences to help spark your wardrobe creativity. Mystery Thrift Boxes are $12 per item plus shipping. Continental US Only.

Wardrobe Consultation

Have pieces in your wardrobe that you're not sure how to wear? The Wardrobe Consultation option is the answer! We'll go through your wardrobe to put together new outfit combinations to help spark your creativity when you're getting dressed. Wardrobe Consultations can be done virtually! This service is $50 for an hour and a half session.

Personal Shopping

Looking for some specific new pieces to fill the holes in your wardrobe? With the Personal Shopping option, we will find styles in your size through secondhand sites or sustainable fashion brands. This option differs from the Mystery Thrift Box option because it offers more control and higher-end pieces. You will be provided with photos and links to the pieces we've found for you based off of your stated budget. Personal Shopping is available for $40 for two hours.
